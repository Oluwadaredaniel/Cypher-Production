## PRODUCT HUNT PAGE COPY

### Tagline
"Control your entire PC from your phone. 100% local."

### Description
**Control Your PC From Your Phone**
We spent months building CYPHER because we got tired of remote desktop latency and uploading files to the cloud just to get them to our own devices.

**CYPHER is:**
- **100% Local**: No cloud, no servers. Just your phone + PC on WiFi.
- **Instant**: Sub-100ms latency for everything.
- **Private**: Your files never leave your network.
- **Open Source**: Audit the code, contribute, fork it.

### Features

**CORE EXPERIENCE**
- **Instant Local Discovery**: Auto-find your PC on the network using mDNS (zero config).
- **Secure 6-Digit Pairing**: One-time handshake with cryptographically secure tokens.
- **Unified Activity Log**: Real-time audit of every command, file, and connection.

**REMOTE CONTROL**
- **Power Management**: Instant shutdown, restart, sleep, and workstation lock.
- **App Launcher**: Browse your Start Menu and launch PC apps from your phone.
- **Process Manager**: Monitor PC CPU/RAM usage per process and kill unresponsive apps.
- **Media Hub**: Play/Pause, Skip, and Volume control for all Windows media players.

**SYNC & TRANSFER**
- **High-Speed File Transfer**: Bidirectional local file streaming with no size limits.
- **Collision-Free Clipboard**: View PC clipboard and sync Phone text to PC instantly.
- **System Monitoring**: Live graphs for CPU/RAM usage and battery status notifications.

**SECURITY & SHARING**
- **Guest QR Access**: Share specific folders with others via self-destructing QR sessions.
- **100% Local**: No cloud, no external servers, no tracking. Your data stays in your room.
